What this is — and isn't

  • Real rollouts, ratchet MAE. Numbers are the mean absolute error on the clean future over the constrained fields, for this held-out patient. Baseline = the shipped coupled x(t) model; TS-JEPA = the denoised-anchor variant, both constraint-valid (0 violations).
  • The noise win comes from a denoised anchor. TS-JEPA forecasts from a learned clean estimate of the current state inferred from the whole window — not the raw noisy reading the baseline is forced to trust. That's the mechanism, and an ablation reverting to the raw anchor collapses the gain (D29).
  • Honest bounds. Seed-0 illustrative (headline numbers are 3-seed gated); the noise win needs noise-augmented training; staleness is patient-dependent (aggregate crossover ~12–15 mo); and it all still lives inside one generator — the generalise-vs-invert ceiling holds.
